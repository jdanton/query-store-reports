# Security Policy

For reporting security vulnerabilities, please contact: joey@joeydantoni.com

## Reporting a Vulnerability
To report a vulnerability, please send an email to joey@joeydantoni.com. Include a description of the vulnerability, potential impact, and steps to reproduce.

## Guidelines

- Do not disclose vulnerabilities publicly before the project maintainers are made aware. 
- Provide enough detail for us to understand the issue and take appropriate measures.
- We appreciate your effort in improving our security and will treat all submissions with respect and confidentiality.
